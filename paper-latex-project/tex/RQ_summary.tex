We organize the study around three research questions. The first quantifies performance gaps, the second explains them, and the third evaluates whether they can be reduced through targeted changes.

\noindent\textbf{RQ1 (Characterization).} What performance gap separates Triton and TileLang kernels from cuBLAS and cuDNN baselines, and how does this gap differ across operator categories?
Our primary measurements use an NVIDIA A100-SXM4-40GB (sm\_80); cross-architecture replay on A100-PCIE-40GB and H100-80GB-HBM3 is reported in \cref{sec:threats}, confirming that the RC mechanisms identified in RQ2 transfer across form factors and GPU families.

\medskip\noindent\textbf{RQ2 (Root-Cause Analysis).} What compiler and architectural factors contribute most to the observed gaps?

\medskip\noindent\textbf{RQ3 (Mitigation).} How much performance can be recovered by targeted implementation fixes derived from the root-cause analysis?
